As many of you may know I am a Jeweler, Metalsmith, Blacksmith, and Bladesmith. I am also a leather craftsman, amateur lapidary artist, and soon to be LaunchCode Graduate!

First of all, I'd like to point out something about my Knifemaking Fun Fact: is that my last name is STAHL which is German and translated means STEEL. Now if you take the similar letters in both words you get STL - which is where I currently live!

A LITTLE BACKGROUND...

  • 1980 - born in Seoul, South Korea.
  • 1980 - Adopted by Stahl Family, Southern Indiana Farmers.
  • 1998 - Graduated High School.
  • 1998-2003 - Obtained BFA in Jewelry Design and Metalsmithing with Minor in Art History.
  • 2003-2015 - Lived in NYC as an Artist, HR Coordinator, Prop Sylist, Production Assistant, Executive Admin to Goldman Sachs Investment Bankers, and more
  • 2015-current - Moved to St. Louis, Missouri and currently lives with her boyfriend and dog.

𝔸s a very curious child growing up I constantly was asking my father about how things were made, how they were built, or in general how they worked. I think I was about 6 years old when I was given a screw driver and an old telephone. Opening it up, performing surgery on it, it was the first time I saw a circuit board - thinking to myself that it looked like a miniature city - and it made me feel like some kind of explorer. It was that feeling mixed with learning about tools and discovering ways to use them; the quality time spent with my father and the potential to make almost anything I found most enjoyable. That is definitely why I continue to enjoy creating and working with my hands.
