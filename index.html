A LITTLE BACKGROUND...

  • 1980 - born in Seoul, South Korea.
  • 1980 - Adopted by Stahl Family, Southern Indiana Farmers.
  • 1998 - Graduated High School.
  • 1998-2003 - Obtained BFA in Jewelry Design and Metalsmithing with Minor in Art History.
  • 2003-2015 - Lived in NYC as an Artist, HR Coordinator, Prop Sylist, Production Assistant, Executive Admin to Goldman Sachs Investment Bankers, and more...
  • 2007-2010 - Began intro to Blacksmithing Classes with instructor Marsha Trattner at She-Weld Studios in Redhook Brooklyn, Became her apprentice the following years.
  • 2015-current - Moved to St. Louis, Missouri and currently lives with her boyfriend and dog.
  • 2016 - First Female Contestant on History Channel's Forged in Fire. Season 2 Episode 1

𝔸s a very curious child growing up I constantly was asking my father about how things were made, how they were built, or in general how they worked. I think I was about 6 years old when I was given a screw driver and an old telephone.
